Celebrating Basant Panchami

Vasant Panchmi, commonly referred to as Basant Panchmi, is a Hindu festival that marks the beginning of the spring season. As per the Hindu calendar, it is celebrated on the Panchami Tithi (fifth day) of the Magh month, which corresponds to the January/February month as in the Gregorian calendar.
